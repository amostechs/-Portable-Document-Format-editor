📄 My PDF Editor (Add Text)

Ready. Load a PDF to preview the first page.
💡 How it works: The app loads your PDF, renders the first page with PDF.js. When you click "Add Text & Download", it uses pdf-lib to embed your text into the actual PDF content (not just an overlay). The modified PDF is then downloaded – and you can re-upload it to see the changes.

⚠️ Note: Coordinates start from bottom‑left corner of the page. Typical A4 page is 595x842 points. Use the preview to experiment with positions.
